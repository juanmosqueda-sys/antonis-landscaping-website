Common questions

Frequently asked.

How much does regular landscape maintenance cost?
Pricing depends on lot size, scope, and frequency, but most of our residential maintenance plans start around $120 per visit for bi-weekly service on a standard LA lot. We give a firm written quote after a free on-site visit so there are no surprises.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. We're a California-licensed contractor (CSLB #768466), fully insured, and carry workers' compensation for every member of our crew. We're happy to send proof of insurance before any job.
Do you help with the LADWP turf replacement rebate?
Yes. We design the landscape, handle the install, and guide you through the LADWP application so you can claim the rebate. Ask us for a free rebate-eligibility check on your yard.
How fast can you come out?
In most of LA County we can schedule a free estimate within 2–3 business days and start routine maintenance the week after. Installs and design projects are booked a few weeks out depending on scope.
Do you service commercial and HOA properties?
Yes. We maintain multifamily, HOA common areas, retail plazas, and small office parks across LA County. We'll walk the property with your board or property manager and put together a tailored monthly proposal with fixed pricing.
Are your crews bilingual?
Yes. English and Spanish on every crew, so whether you want to chat with the foreman on site or get a written proposal, communication is never an issue.
